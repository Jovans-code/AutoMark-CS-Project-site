How to Use AutoMark

  1. Install the AutoMark App on your mobile device.
  2. Sign up or log in using your email address.
  3. Go to the Upload Script section and upload clear images of your students' scripts.
  4. Navigate to the Answer Key section and upload the marking guide for the exam or assignment.
  5. Open the Unmarked Scripts section to see all uploaded student scripts ready for marking.
  6. Tap the Mark button on any script card to begin the marking process.
  7. Choose your preferred marking method: Auto Mark or AI Mark.
  8. Let AutoMark compare student responses against the guide and generate scores with detailed feedback.
  9. View all marked results in the Results section, complete with feedback and marking method used.
