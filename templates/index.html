How to Use AutoMark

  1. Install the AutoMark App on your mobile device.
  2. Sign up or log in using your email address.
  3. Go to the Upload Script section and upload clear images of your students' scripts.
  4. Navigate to the Answer Key section and upload the marking guide for the exam or assignment.
  5. Open the Unmarked Scripts section to view scripts ready for marking.
  6. Tap the Mark button to begin grading. Choose either Auto Mark or AI Mark.
  7. View all marked results in the Results section, complete with feedback and marking method used.
  8. Upgrade to Premium to unlock powerful features like:
    • Bulk Marking of multiple scripts at once
    • Analytics Dashboard for performance insights
    • Advanced PDF Reports for easy sharing
    • Long-Term Storage of marked scripts
  9. Go to the Payments section in the app to upgrade securely via MTN Mobile Money.
