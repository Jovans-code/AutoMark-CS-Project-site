Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 17, 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and authentication credentials.
  • App Usage Data: Interactions with the AutoMark app, grading results, and scan details.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and IP address (for analytics and security).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To analyze and troubleshoot technical issues
  • To communicate important updates or changes
  • To enhance the accuracy of our grading algorithms

3. Sharing of Data

We do not share your personal data with third parties, except when legally required or to provide essential services (e.g., Firebase authentication). Student grading data is never shared with unauthorized parties.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmissions
  • Secure storage with access controls
  • Regular security audits
  • Two-factor authentication options

5. Your Rights

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Lodge complaints with regulatory authorities

6. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@automark.app or through our contact form.
